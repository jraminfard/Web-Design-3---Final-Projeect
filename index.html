FAQ

1. Do you offer international shipping?

2. How much does each swatch cost?
The first three swatches are free. After that, each swatch costs. $.50.

3. What if I saw a swatch I really liked in-store but can't find it online?
If you see something you love in-store, it is best to buy it right away because it might be a discontinued item. If you can't find what you love online, give our Customer Service team a call and they will be happy to assist you in finding a similar fabric to the one you had your eyes set on!
